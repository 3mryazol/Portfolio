## Projects

### 🌙 Noor — Islamic Knowledge Infrastructure
*An open, non-profit project to organize and preserve Islamic knowledge as reusable infrastructure — not another app, but the foundation other things can be built on.*

The core problem Noor tries to solve: authentic Islamic knowledge — Qur'an, Sunnah, tafsir, hadith, scholarly works — already exists, but it's scattered across PDFs, websites, lectures, and personal notes. It's not that the knowledge is hard to find; it's that it's disconnected.

Noor treats **knowledge as the canonical product**, and everything else — presentations, podcasts, apps, AI tools, websites — as an interface generated *from* that single source, never edited independently of it.

Two design principles anchor it:
- **Authority hierarchy** — Qur'an and authentic Sunnah sit at the top; tafsir, scholars, and madhhabs derive from and explain them, never stand equal to them.
- **Traceability** — every claim has to answer *where did this come from*, and must be checked against the Qur'an as understood through authentic Hadith and Sunnah before it counts as valid.

This is early-stage (working draft v0.1), but it's the project I'm most personally invested in — it sits right at the intersection of my interests in knowledge systems, trustworthy AI, and building things that last longer than a trend.
